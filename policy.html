Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, protect, and use the data you share during emergency interactions on this platform.

Personal Data Collection

We collect only essential information such as your name, contact details, location, and emergency messages. This data is used strictly to validate your identity and help us respond effectively to emergencies.

You are never required to share sensitive or financial information. We follow a minimal data policy, gathering only what’s necessary for communication and verification.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used to verify reports, coordinate disaster responses, and communicate with relevant authorities. For example, your location helps us dispatch emergency support precisely where it’s needed.

We do not use your data for advertising or promotions. We will never sell, trade, or distribute your personal information to third parties outside of emergency coordination channels.

Security and Access

All collected data is securely stored using modern encryption and access controls. Only authorized personnel involved in emergency operations can access your information.

You may contact us at any time to view, update, or delete your personal data. We support full transparency and give users the right to request removal of their records upon completion of a case or situation.
